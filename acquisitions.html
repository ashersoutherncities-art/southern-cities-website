Deal Sourcing, Simplified

Finding great real estate deals is time-consuming, competitive, and often frustrating. You're competing with hundreds of other investors for the same MLS listings, paying wholesalers inflated prices, or spending countless hours searching for off-market opportunities.

Our acquisitions division eliminates the noise. We operate a multi-channel deal sourcing machine that combines county records scraping, pre-foreclosure monitoring, cold calling campaigns, and proprietary lead sources to surface opportunities before they hit the market.

Every deal we send you includes professional analysis: ARV estimates, repair budgets, comparable sales, and clear ROI projections. We're active investors ourselves—we understand your criteria because we use the same standards for our own acquisitions.

Our Investment Criteria

🏚️

Rehab Projects

30-50% of ARV

We target distressed properties with strong upside potential. After-Repair Value (ARV) must support at least 15%+ profit margin after all costs.

🏗️

Development Land

≤20% of As-Built Value

Raw land or teardown opportunities priced at 20% or less of stabilized project value. Focuses on highest-and-best-use potential.
