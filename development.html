Our Development Process

Phase 1: Opportunity Identification

Site assessment, market research, and preliminary feasibility analysis. We determine if the project aligns with investment criteria and market demand.

Phase 2: Financial Modeling

Detailed proforma development, cost estimation, and ROI projections. Scenario analysis to stress-test assumptions and identify risks.

Phase 3: Site Control & Due Diligence

Acquisition or option agreements, environmental studies, geotechnical surveys, title work, and zoning verification.

Phase 4: Design & Entitlement

Architectural design, civil engineering, site plan approvals, and permit acquisition. Coordinated with local planning departments.

Phase 5: Financing & Construction

Construction loan closing, GC selection (or self-perform via our GC division), and active construction with weekly oversight.

Phase 6: Completion & Exit

Final inspections, certificate of occupancy, lease-up or sale coordination, and project closeout. Refinance or disposition as planned.
