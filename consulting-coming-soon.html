Frequently Asked Questions

What if I need to pause my retainer?
We understand business needs change. You can pause your retainer for up to 3 months with 30 days notice. Unused hours do not roll over, but we're flexible with scheduling within each month.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?
Absolutely. You can upgrade at any time and the change takes effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle. We want you in the right package for your current needs.
How quickly can I get started?
Most clients start within 7 days of signing up. We'll schedule your onboarding call within 48 hours and begin working together immediately. For Enterprise and Platinum clients, we can start same-week.
What if I'm not satisfied?
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on your first month. If you're not seeing value, we'll refund your payment — no questions asked. Our client retention rate is 87%, so we're confident you'll see results.
Can consulting hours be used for other SCE services?
Yes! Retainer hours can be applied to any Southern Cities Enterprises service at the equivalent hourly rate. This gives you flexibility to use consulting time for deal analysis, project management support, or strategic planning.
Do you work with out-of-state investors?
Yes. While we're based in Charlotte, about 40% of our retainer clients are out-of-state investors. Most consulting happens via video calls, with quarterly in-person sessions for Enterprise+ clients.
