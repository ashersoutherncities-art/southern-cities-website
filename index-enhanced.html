Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Charlotte metro area and surrounding counties in North Carolina and South Carolina. For larger projects or investor partnerships, we can extend our services to other markets.

How quickly can you provide a cost estimate?

Our turnaround times vary by package: Basic Scope estimates take 1-2 days, Detailed Estimates take 3-5 days, and Comprehensive Packages take 5-7 days. Rush service is available for an additional fee.

Do you work with out-of-state investors?

Absolutely! Many of our clients are out-of-state investors. We provide full project management, regular photo updates, and detailed reporting so you can invest remotely with confidence.

What's included in the deal sourcing subscription?

Our deal sourcing service provides pre-screened properties that match your investment criteria, including ARV analysis, repair estimates, and profitability projections. Higher tiers include custom deal matching and full project coordination.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We hold a General Contractor license and Real Estate Broker license in North Carolina. We carry comprehensive liability insurance, workers' compensation, and professional indemnity coverage for all our divisions.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes. All subscription services can be cancelled with 30 days' notice. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees.
